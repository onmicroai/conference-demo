Clinical Simulation

In the sample simulation app, you'll play the role of a clinician speaking to a patient. You'll be assessed on how well you can do the following:

  • 1
    Introduce your name and your role
  • 2
    Ask the patient where the pain is located
  • 3
    Ask the patient to rate the pain on a scale of 1 to 10

You will only have a short conversation (5 turns) to complete these tasks. Once completed, you will have a chance to make a diagnosis and receive feedback.
